About Me

Audio Engineer Over 10 Years of Experience .

My journey in the world of audio began in 2011, driven by passion, experimentation, and relentless work. I started with simple tools, but mastering the craft and respecting its finest details have always been my primary motivation.

Moving between freelance projects and field experience over the years, I have contributed to a vast range of productions, including films, recorded programs, and audio content.

Gradually, I took on responsibilities for recording, mixing, and mastering for leading content companies such as:
Thmanyah (working on documentaries and podcasts), MBC, Alif Alif FM, Makkah TV, Oreb, and others.

Today, I possess over a decade of experience in handling audio as an impactful message rather than just a technical element.

I constantly strive to evolve my tools and skills, seeking to work within professional environments that value quality and believe in the power of content.

I recently graduated from the Audio Institute of America in San Francisco, USA, earning a Diploma in Recording Engineering and Layout Techniques.
