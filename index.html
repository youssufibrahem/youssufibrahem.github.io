About Me

Audio Engineer

Education & Certifications
  • Diploma in Recording Engineering and Audio Technology | Audio Institute of America (AIA), San Francisco, USA.
  • Advanced Mixing & Mastering Course | Gulf Media Studios, Kuwait.
Professional Journey

My journey in the world of sound began in 2011, driven by pure passion, experimentation, and relentless dedication. Starting with simple tools, my obsession with mastering the craft and respecting its finest details propelled me to advanced levels of audio engineering.
Over the years, my experience has spanned freelance and field-based projects, contributing to a diverse range of productions including films, TV series, recorded programs, and professional audio content.

Key Roles & Collaborations

I specialize in Recording, Mixing, and Mastering for leading media organizations and content creators, including:

  • Thmanyah (Documentaries and Podcasts)
  • MBC Group
  • Al-Thakafiya TV (Cultural Channel)
  • Saudi TV 
  • Makkah TV
  • Alif Alif FM
  • Areebe Company, and more.
Vision

Today, with over a decade of experience, I view sound not merely as a technical element, but as a powerful message. I am constantly evolving my tools and skills, seeking to work within professional environments that prioritize quality and believe in the transformative power of content.
